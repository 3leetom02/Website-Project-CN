About this site

This website was created for the purpose of cataloging and reviewing the media I like, in order to allow others some insight into my tastes. Throughout the site you will find links to various pages on different topics.

I have decided to include pages for film, music and books as I feel that these are the topics I can write most about. You will find that the pages will include various types of content such as lists,reviews and a general overview of the topic discusssed.

Here is an overview of what you will find on this website

Film Page

Here you can view a list of my top ten films of all time as well as my review of Stanley Kubrick's classic The Shining.

Music Page

Here you will find a chart of my top albums of all time. You will also find my article 'The Importance of Post-Punk which is my attempt to provide an introduction to the genre.'

I hope you enjoy my website.
